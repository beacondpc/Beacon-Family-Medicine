Dr. Beryl Kalul

Dr. Kalul is a board certified family medicine physician who is happy to call North Texas home. She believes that the modern system of medicine is itself quite sick, and that the direct primary care (DPC) model is the cure. Dr. Kalul doesn't just want to know your symptoms, medications and illnesses – she wants to know YOU, and to show you the difference between spending ten minutes with your doctor versus having a real relationship with a doctor who has the time to show you she truly cares.
