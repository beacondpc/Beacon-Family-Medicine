Meet Dr. Beryl Kalul

Dr. Beryl Kalul is a board certified family medicine physician who is happy to call North Texas home. She believes that the modern system of medicine is itself very sick, and that the direct primary care (DPC) model is the cure. Dr. Kalul doesn't just want to know your symptoms, medications and illnesses – she wants to know YOU, and to show you the difference between spending 10 minutes with your doctor versus having a real relationship with your doctor.

Why families choose DPC

Traditional insurance complicates primary care. DPC restores access and trust with a simple membership. Fewer patients per doctor means longer visits, same‑ or next‑day scheduling, and direct messaging when you need help.
